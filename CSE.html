B Tech

Computer Science Engineering (CSE) is a professional undergraduate engineering degree programme awarded to candidates after they complete four years of study in the field.


Engineering is one of the most popular courses in India and there are many institutes that offer the course to aspiring students.
This increasing growth of IT industry has created a lot of opportunities for the computer graduates.


Bachelor of Technology is one of the popular courses among the students who want to make their career in the Engineering field.


The duration of the course is 4 years and divided into 8 semesters.
It comprises of the subjects like database, networking, data structure, core programming languages like ‘C’ and ‘java’ and many other technical things.
